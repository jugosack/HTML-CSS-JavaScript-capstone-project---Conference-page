MilCon 2023

MilCon was initiated as a one-day symposium in 2015 led by a International Institute for Development and Research Dr. Jugoslav Achkoski (2IDRJA) In 2018, 2IDRJA transformed MilCon into a two-day professional conference that attracted attendees from across the country. Following its success, Military Academy asked to partner with 2IDRJA and the two organisations are now working together to alternate the conference between campuses yearly.
